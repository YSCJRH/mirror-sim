# East Wharf Dispatch Notes

Dispatch runners reported confusion between the public works office, the archive room, and the mayor's staff. One runner wrote that Su He wanted the maintenance record surfaced immediately, while another said Zhao Ke still wanted the festival opening bell to proceed on schedule.

The dispatch board confirms that Lin Lan has a document connecting the gate repair delay to the budget transfer. It also records that messengers are already taking longer routes because of crowd barricades near the harbor square.

A final note warns that if communication slows any further, East Wharf residents may hear about the flood risk after the storm surge is already visible from the quay.
