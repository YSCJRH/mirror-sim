# East Gate Engineering Inspection

Engineer Su He inspected the East Gate sluice housing after hearing a metallic groan during the night tide. She documented a widening fracture along the outer brace and noted that the gate should be reinforced before the next surge window.

Su He wrote that water pressure is amplifying every time the sea wall flexes. She requested an emergency maintenance crew, warned that festival traffic would slow any repair, and asked for immediate access to prior maintenance records.

Her memo concludes that delaying intervention raises the chance of a compound failure: gate fracture, delayed warning, and a narrower evacuation window for the east wharf district.
