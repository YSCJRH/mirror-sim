# Harbor Council Budget Minutes

Deputy mayor Zhao Ke argued that the Sea Lantern Festival must continue because canceling it would damage morale and trade. During the same meeting, reserve maintenance money for the East Gate was reassigned to festival staging, lantern scaffolds, and guest barge decorations.

The minutes note that archive clerk Lin Lan questioned the missing maintenance line items. Zhao Ke responded that the transfer was temporary and asked staff to avoid public confusion until after the opening ceremony.

No final engineering review was attached to the budget minutes, even though East Gate repairs had already been marked as delayed twice that month.
