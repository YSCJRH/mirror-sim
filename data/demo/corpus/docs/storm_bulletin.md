# Harbor Observatory Storm Bulletin

Fog Harbor will face a fast storm front by tomorrow night. The observatory expects high tide surges strong enough to test every flood gate on the eastern wall.

Captain Chen Yu already reported unusual chop near the East Gate channel before sunrise. If the gate is compromised, low-lying streets east of the festival grounds could flood with very little warning.

The bulletin recommends reviewing gate integrity, checking evacuation routes, and preparing to suspend public gatherings if engineering reports confirm structural damage.
