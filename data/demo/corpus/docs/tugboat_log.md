# Tide Log From The Tug Lantern Three

Captain Chen Yu recorded an abnormal pull under the eastern hull while crossing the channel before dawn. He saw spray bursting through the East Gate seam and wrote that the gate shuddered harder than it had during the last storm season.

Chen Yu warned the harbor office that cargo crews would have trouble clearing the quay if the festival crowd blocked the road. He added that a delayed evacuation order would trap carts and lantern barges in the same lane.

His closing note says he trusts Su He more than the mayor's office on water risk because she has been measuring the gate directly.
