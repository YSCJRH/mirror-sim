# Copy Of The Maintenance Ledger

Lin Lan copied the East Gate maintenance ledger before the archive room closed for the day. The copied pages show that repair funds approved for brace replacement were redirected to festival construction invoices signed by the deputy mayor's office.

She prepared the copy for a courier so that a local reporter could publish the discrepancy if the council tried to hide it. Lin Lan believed the ledger would force a decision before the storm arrived.

The copy also references repeated repair deferrals and the exact work order that Su He requested after her latest inspection.
